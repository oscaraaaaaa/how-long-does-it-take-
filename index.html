Health and Recovery: How Long Does It Take to Heal?

How Long Does It Take to Get Over a Cold?

The common cold typically lasts 7 to 10 days. However, it can vary depending on your immune system and the severity of symptoms.


  • Mild cases: 3–5 days

  • Moderate: Up to a week

  • Severe: 10+ days

How Long Does It Take to Heal a Broken Bone?

Bone healing depends on the location and severity of the break:


  • Small fractures: 6–8 weeks

  • Major breaks: 3–6 months

  • Elderly or health-compromised individuals may take longer

How Long Does It Take to Recover from Surgery?

Surgical recovery varies widely:


  • Minor procedures (e.g., mole removal): a few days

  • Appendectomy: 2–4 weeks

  • Joint replacements: 3–6 months

Always follow your doctor’s instructions for optimal healing.

Travel and Commutes: Timing Your Journeys

How Long Does It Take to Fly Across the U.S.?

A non-stop flight from New York to Los Angeles takes about 6 hours. With layovers, total travel time may stretch to 8–10 hours.

How Long Does It Take to Drive 100 Miles?

Assuming a steady speed of 60 mph, it would take about 1 hour and 40 minutes. But road conditions, traffic, and speed limits matter too.

How Long Does It Take to Get a Passport?

In the U.S.:


  • Standard processing: 8–11 weeks

  • Expedited: 5–7 weeks

  • Emergency travel: Within 72 hours (with proof)

Food and Cooking: Timing Your Recipes

How Long Does It Take to Boil an Egg?


  • Soft-boiled: 4–6 minutes

  • Medium-boiled: 7–9 minutes

  • Hard-boiled: 10–12 minutes

Use a timer for perfect results every time.

How Long Does It Take to Cook Chicken?

Cooking time depends on the cut and method:


  • Boneless breasts (oven): 20–25 minutes at 400°F

  • Whole chicken (roast): 1–1.5 hours

  • Grilled thighs: 15–20 minutes

Use a meat thermometer: safe internal temperature is 165°F (74°C).

Education and Learning: How Long Does It Take to Learn Something New?

How Long Does It Take to Learn a Language?

It depends on your dedication and the language’s complexity:


  • Basic fluency in Spanish or French: 6–12 months (1 hour/day)

  • Advanced fluency: 2–3 years

  • Difficult languages (e.g., Chinese, Arabic): 3–5 years

Consistency is key.

How Long Does It Take to Get a Degree?


  • Associate degree: 2 years

  • Bachelor’s degree: 4 years

  • Master’s degree: 1–2 years

  • PhD: 3–7 years

Online programs may be faster.

Fitness and Weight Loss: Timing Your Progress

How Long Does It Take to Lose Weight?

Safe weight loss is 1–2 pounds per week. So:


  • 5 pounds: 3–5 weeks

  • 20 pounds: 3–5 months

Crash diets can be harmful. Focus on consistency and health.

How Long Does It Take to Build Muscle?

Noticeable muscle gain takes 8–12 weeks with:

  • Regular strength training
  • Protein-rich diet
  • Proper rest

Progress varies by age, genetics, and effort.

Tech and Digital Life

How Long Does It Take to Set Up a Website?


  • Basic site (e.g., WordPress): A few hours

  • Custom-built site: Several weeks to months

Don’t forget about domain registration and hosting setup time.

Life Milestones: From Babies to Careers

How Long Does It Take to Have a Baby?

Pregnancy lasts about 40 weeks (9 months). Labor and delivery can range from:


  • First-time moms: 12–24 hours

  • Subsequent births: 6–12 hours

How Long Does It Take to Get a Job?

From application to hiring:


  • Average: 3–6 weeks

  • Competitive fields: Up to 3 months

Networking and tailored resumes help shorten the process.

How Long Does It Take to Start a Business?

From idea to launch:


  • Small business: 3–6 months

  • Tech startup: 6–18 months

  • Franchise: 2–4 months

Include time for legal setup, market research, and funding.

Miscellaneous "How Long Does It Take" Questions

How Long Does It Take to Charge an Electric Car?


  • Level 1 charger (standard outlet): 8–12 hours

  • Level 2 charger (240V): 4–6 hours

  • Fast charging: 30–60 minutes

How Long Does It Take for Eyelashes to Grow Back?


  • Shedding to regrowth: 6–8 weeks

  • Full replacement: 2–3 months

Avoid rubbing your eyes or using harsh makeup removers.

How Long Does It Take for a Tattoo to Heal?


  • Initial healing: 7–14 days

  • Full healing: 4–6 weeks

Keep the area clean and moisturized. Avoid sun exposure.
